Sherlock, The Basset Hound

Bringing joy to everyone's lives since 2010
During his 8 years he has played a lot, walked a lot, barked, jumped, stolen food items (and non-food items), but most of all he has brought smiles to a lot of people's faces!

    So many adventures:

  • Born in Cachoeirinha, a small city in southern Brazil;
  • A few months after birth, flew to Rio de Janeiro to meet his new human parents, Agata e Fabrício;
  • Made a lot of new friends at home while waiting for his first vaccinations before he could go for a stroll;
  • Finally could go outside, but as a proper Basset Hound, always refused to walk too much and just sat by the curb after a few steps;
  • Loved the pub near his house, where he would sit and charm the drunken men to pet him;
  • Went to the beach many times (Ipanema, Copabana, Barra). He loves the beach, but there's no way he would go close to the water;
  • His favorite hobby is travelling by car. He just sits there and sleeps, knowing his humans are less than a meter away;
  • Made a few trips to Porto Alegre, close to his hometown, a few times. It's a trip of about 1,000 miles;
  • Moved to Porto Alegre in 2015, where he currently lives;
  • This year he lost his good friend Nana, a lovely Beagle, to cancer. He couldn't get more sad;
  • In a few months he will embark in his biggest adventure. He is going to Poland with his humans to enjoy the wonders of the winter in the northern hemisfere. And a few pierogi too, hopefully!

"I have never met a more interesting and joyful creature!"

-- Everyone that crosses his path
